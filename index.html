Owner / Operator

Emily Hirst began Celestial Rose in February 2023, after bartending weddings and special events for over 4 years. Her passion of creating new cocktail flavors for friends and family gave her the confidence and idea to do it professionally. Along with her husband, Scott, they are excited to help others elevate their bar services when hosting a special occasion.

Our Team

Each member of our team is alcohol certified and liquor-liability insured, with years of experience. Whether pouring wine or mixing a margarita, each teammate brings their own personality and style.

What we do

Email us for more information and pricing for the below packages/services.

Basic Bartending

Want to keep it simple with only beer and wine? Our basic bartending package is best for those looking to only have bartenders. Minimum 3 hours of service, maximum 8 hours for up to 250 guests.

Basic Day-Of Coordination

We know your wedding day can be stressful. With our basic package, we use your instructions and timeline to ensure vendors and guests are on time and in place for your ceremony and reception. Maximum 9 hours.

Full Bartending

This package is for those wanting mixed drinks, in addition to beer and wine. Minimum 3 hours of service, maximum 8 hours for up to 250 guests. Two bartenders required.

Week-Of Coordination

During the week of your wedding, we work one-on-one with you and your vendors to fine-tune your wedding day. We will help create a timeline for the day-of, as well as any other events leading up to it.

Signature Cocktail + Full Bar

Want to personalize your cocktail list? Look no further. This package comes with everything listed in the "Full Bartending" package above, as well as up to three signature cocktails crafted by Emily. Minimum 4 months booking before event date.
